Casting Material Choices

For our casting materials, we decided that the hardness of the material would be the most important property, so we selected a variety of hardnesses, and a mold release agent to go with them.
They had thefollowing Shore hardness:
Silicone Rubber - 10A
Urethane Rubber - 60A
Urethane Resin - 80D
The exact product we used is avalible at: https://www.smooth-on.com/products/

Molding Material Choices

4 Molding Materials were used they are:
PLA - Basic/default material, cheap and generally well rounded material.
PETG - common material with some different material properties to PLA (such as heat resistance, layer adhesion, and surface finish).
TPU - A flexible material, while it's harder to print with the flex might make mold releasing easier.
ASA - We want to use ASA as it is known for it's ability to be smoothed with the use of acetone, which might help get better casts that release easier with eliminated layer lines and its high heat resistance.
